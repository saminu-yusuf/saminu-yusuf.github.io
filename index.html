Project 2: Carelocator

After going through all the critical stages of planning, ideation, and design, we are now ready to move into the development phase. This journey has been both challenging and rewarding, and we're confident that our solution will address the problem of doctor availability in hospitals during emergencies. Together, we've laid the foundation for CareLocator, and now, with development as our final stage, we are ready to bring this solution to life and make a positive impact in healthcare accessibility.
